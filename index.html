8 Simples Rules

  1. Four Contestants

    2. You must provide at least 50 signatures

  2. Four Hours

    3. Starting at 8pm and finishing at 12am

  3. Twelve Nights

    4. Spread over four weekends

  4. Eight Locations

    5. You may choose any pub from the selection, but spend at least one night in each

  5. Six Drinks Options

    6. Choose from a selection of drinks, but you must consume the same drink throughout the night

  6. Twelve Costumes

    7. Each night a new costume will be provided for you and must be worn

  7. You're Out

    8. Passing out during the night is a disqualification

  8. Have Fun!

    9. Drink Responsibly
